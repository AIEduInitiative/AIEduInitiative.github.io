Who We Are

AI Educational Initiative


The AI Educational Initiative was founded by a group of curious scientists with a deep interest in global AI breakthroughs. The founders are connected by the following shared goals:

  1. Educating themselves and others about the practical and positive applications of AI for the betterment of society and the environment. This education will be conducted in various languages, using basic scientific communication methods.

  2. Adhering to legal regulations concerning AI and advocating for appropriate and necessary regulations in collaboration with other initiatives.

  3. Advocating for free and unrestricted access to AI for everyone.

  4. Raising awareness about the issues related to data protection when using AI and emphasizing the need to prioritize privacy protection.

  5. Organizing debates on AI and ethics to foster discussions and critical thinking on the responsible use of AI.

  6. Amplifying the influence of AI, law, and ethics experts’ opinions in AI regulation debates. Establishing educational, awareness, and support programs to address concerns that people may have about losing jobs, experiencing a decrease in their sense of purpose and self-worth, or facing lowered motivation due to AI advancements.

  7. Encouraging authorities to create legislation and social programs that facilitate individuals in adapting to a world with AI, including learning new skills, finding new jobs, and maintaining motivation and drive. Advocating for the equitable redistribution of wealth generated by AI throughout society.

  8. As a long-term objective, utilizing AI for scientific projects, such as environmental sustainability and biomedical initiatives.

  9. By pursuing these aims, the AI Educational Initiative seeks to foster a responsible and inclusive AI landscape that benefits all of humanity.
